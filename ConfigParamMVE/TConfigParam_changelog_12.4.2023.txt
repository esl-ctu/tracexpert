TConfigParam changelog 12.4.2023
  Readonly parameters
      - isReadonly method
  TType changes:
      - TDouble renamed as TReal; double changed to qreal (Typedef for double unless Qt is configured with the -qreal float option.)
      - New type TFileName, alias for TString
      - New type TTime, alias for TReal
  Warning changes:
      - Warning methods (and members) deleted
      - TState enum
      - setState, resetState, getState, getStateMessage methods added
